This is a bit about me

After years spent home with my kids, a short dabble in teaching, and the support of my wife and little ones, I am finally chasing after my dream of becoming a developer.

I have always loved programming, and it's been my go-to for when I am stressed, bored, or just want something enjoyable to do. I am currently working toward two more certifications in iOS and Swift, while continuing to refine my skills in front-end and back-end web development.

I took a huge risk in quitting my teaching job, but what better time to pursue my dream than in my mid-30s with a wife and two kids to provide for?

Fingers crossed this works out and my code doesn't suck.
