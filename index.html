About

After years spent home with my kids, a short dabble in teaching, and the support of my wife and little ones, I am finally chasing after my dream of becoming a developer.

I have always loved programming, and it's been my go-to for when I am stressed, bored, or just want something enjoyable to do. I am mostly self-taught, but recently received a certification in iOS 11 and Swift 4, and am knowledgeable in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and a little PHP and SQL. I am currently working toward two more certifications in iOS and Swift, while continuing to refine my skills in front-end and back-end web development.

I took a huge risk in quitting my teaching job, but what better time to pursue my dream than in my mid-30s with a wife and two kids to provide for?

Fingers crossed I can make this work.

Alloc

Budget planning app. Allows past, present, and future budget periods. It allows setting up of categories and paychecks that perseverate across periods, as well as specific budget items that might only occur in a particular budget period.

Uses Auto-layout, ToolBars & NavBars, Multiple Storyboards, UIAlerts, UITapGestureRecognizers, UITableViews with custom UITableCells, Haptics, Protocols & Delegates, and CoreData. It is still a work in progress, but the goal is publishing on the App Store.

UnWeather

Simple weather app that uses Unsplash & OpenWeather APIs. (Hence the name "UnWeather"). Loads 6 random images from Unsplash that match some keywords from the weather description returned from the OpenWeather API as the background.
